Section 1:
WHEREAS, on July 4, 2025, following congressional approval, the President signed into law Public Law 119-21, commonly known as One Big Beautiful Bill Act, 2025;WHEREAS, Public Law 119-21, makes appropriations related to the Rural Health Transformation Program, including appropriations provided to the states through certain federal agencies;WHEREAS, through the Rural Health Transformation Program, the state of Utah is to receive this year federal funds in excess of $10,000,000;WHEREAS, Section 63J-5-204 of the Utah Code requires that the Legislature approve the state's receipt of new federal funds, if the state will receive total payments of $10,000,000 or more per year from the federal government; andWHEREAS, the funds appropriated under the Rural Health Transformation Program are temporary grants, the state of Utah should consider the following guiding principles for use of the funds:(1) initiatives should seek to create sustainable positive financial outcomes without creating future financial obligations for the state or permanently committing the state to a local government or private sector responsibility; (2) initiatives should prioritize one-time projects or upgrades;(3) initiatives should seek to leverage non-state resources where possible by partnering with the private sector or other levels of government;(4) initiatives should inspire innovation in healthcare delivery, including healthcare data and electronic record sharing;(5) initiatives should seek to improve the health outcomes of Utahns; and(6) when awarding funds, the state of Utah should notify funding recipients that the funds are temporary and do not create an ongoing obligation by the state government:NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the Legislature of the state of Utah approves acceptance by the state of Utah of $500,000,000 over five years, beginning in fiscal year 2026, with the possibility of another $500,000,000 over that time period, awarded under the Rural Health Transformation Program granted to the state of Utah under Public Law 119-21, commonly known as One Big Beautiful Bill Act, 2025.BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that a copy of this resolution be sent to the governor and the members of Utah's congressional delegation.
